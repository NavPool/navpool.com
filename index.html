What is NavPool?

NavPool is a shared staking pool operated by the NavCoin community.

We are here to help and support stakers with balances of all sizes to earn NavCoin through the proof of stake mechanism.

By staking your coins with NavPool you'll be able to earn interest on your NavCoin and have full participation with the community fund. Whilst at the same time your NavCoin are kept safe in your private wallet.

NavPool is an initiative fully funded by the NavCoin Community Fund. Our code is 100% open source and published to github.

Secure staking

We are a non custodial staking pool. Your NavCoin remain in your wallet.

Earn interest staking

You can earn up to 10% interest/year staking your NavCoin with NavPool.

Two factor Authentication

Fully protect your NavPool account with two factor authentication (2FA).

Fully anonymous

We are an anonymous staking pool. We do not require any personal data.

Community fund

Vote for your favourite community fund proposals through our online portal.
